About Our Planets

  • Mercury: Closest to the Sun, extreme temperature fluctuations, no moons.
  • Venus: Known for its thick atmosphere and surface temperatures hotter than Mercury's.
  • Earth: The only planet known to support life, with one moon.
  • Mars: Known as the "Red Planet", has the largest volcano in the solar system.
  • Jupiter: The largest planet, has a giant storm called the Great Red Spot.
  • Saturn: Famous for its spectacular ring system.
  • Uranus: Rotates on its side, has faint rings.
  • Neptune: Known for its striking blue color and strong winds.
